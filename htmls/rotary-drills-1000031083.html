Overview

Cat® From the Ground Up. Productive From the Ground Down

The Cat ® MD6250 is built from the ground up with 100% Cat parts to help you get the most out of your operation. With built-in autonomous features and the option to integrate Cat MineStar™ technologies, the MD6250 offers accurate, reliable performance in any drilling application. It’s easy to own and operate, with improved serviceability and the reliable performance you expect from Caterpillar — backed by the world-class support of the Cat dealer network. Designed to drill holes from 152-250 mm (6-9.8 in) in diameter, the MD6250 is is optimized for single-pass or multi-pass drilling in both rotary and down-the-hole drilling modes.
